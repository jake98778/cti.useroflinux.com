Welcome to News CTI Tracker

Thanks for checking out this tool! It’s a Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) collector that pulls news from various cybersecurity sources into one convenient spot.

You can add keywords—like "Windows"—to highlight relevant CVEs and news for products you care about, making CTI tracking a breeze.

Please understand this unreleased and a work in progress and by no means is a public release. If its broken I apologize in advance because I am most likely currently programming a change if its down.
